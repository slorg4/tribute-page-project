Sonic the Hedgehog

The fastest thing alive

Sonic in his classic aspect, he has received changes over his time of existence.

Here are some facts about Sonic the Hedgehog:

  • There have been over 70 million Sonic the Hedgehog games sold globally.
  • The name originally suggested for the Sonic project was “Project Needlemouse”.
  • Over 60 million people have played games based on Sonic the Hedgehog.
  • It has registered itself in the Guinness Book of world records for being the fastest gaming character.
  • In a poll in 2009, it was rated as the number one gaming icon of all times.
  • The shoes that Sonic wears are inspired by Santa Claus’ red shoes.
  • Sonic had a girlfriend known as “Madonna” who featured in a Rockband.
  • He first appeared in an arcade game called Rad Mobile where it was found to be hanging as a toy from the rearview mirror.
  • Sonic appeared in the Disney animated film Wreck-It Ralph.
  • It has also sponsored the Williams Grand Prix team in the 1993 Formula One championship.
  • It was the first gaming icon that had a balloon in Macy’s Thanksgiving day parade.
  • Sonic the Hedgehog has appeared in many video games, films, TV shows, and comic books.
  • In total Sonic has appeared in over an amazing 65 games.
  • Dr. Eggman, his Nemesis in this game series has an IQ of 300.
  • This character was made with the thought in mind that it can never stand boredom.
  • Sonic’s favorite food is chili dogs.
  • Sonic cannot swim.
  • His favorite drink is Cola.
  • The sneakers that Sonic wears were designed by Kintobor before he became evil.
  • Emerald hill zone is the place where Sonic grew up.
